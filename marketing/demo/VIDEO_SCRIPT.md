# 🌌 AETHER TAURI // DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT
## "The Sovereign Neural Workstation"

**Goal:** Highlight the cross-platform desktop experience, the power of local AI, and the unified ecosystem.
**Style:** Cinematic, high-tech, fast-paced but clear.
**Colors:** Cyan (#58a6ff), Purple (#bc8cff), Dark Background (#0d1117).

**Visual:** 
- A dark screen with a glowing "🌌" emoji in the center.
- Fade in text: "Your Hardware. Your AI. Your Rules."
- The Aether Hero SVG logo fades in.
**Audio:** Low bass hum, synth swell.
**Voiceover:** "Your phone already runs Aether. Now, your desktop does too."

**Visual:** 
- Quick cuts of the Aether Tauri UI.
